Silverado Logo Branding Statement
===================================================

After lengthy discussions with the client it has been decided that the "Silverado" name is an established brand with historical significance for the town. Therefor the Silverado name is staying and an overhaul of the logo was chosen instead. The new logo design can be seen across the website.
